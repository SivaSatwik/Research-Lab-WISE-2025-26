## ğŸ“Š Project Overview
This research analyzes attitudes, trust, and ethical concerns regarding ChatGPT among 22,963 higher education students from 120 countries.

### Research Questions
1. What factors predict student trust in ChatGPT?
2. How do ethical concerns vary by usage frequency and discipline?
3. What is the relationship between usage patterns, capabilities, and trust?
4. Do these relationships differ across disciplines?
